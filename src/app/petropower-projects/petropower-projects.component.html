PetroPower Projects

Oil-Pipeline Ticketing System

Truck drivers would be able to submit their tickets via the HMI. The HMI then uploads the ticket information to display on the website for the managers to view. The HMI is connected to VEGAPuls 61 radar level sensor, and TM2700 flow meter. The managers would also be able to shut down his site remotely from Petropower's webpage.

ABB uFlo6213 Gas Monitoring System

Integration between Petropower's system with ABB uFlo6213. The monitoring system transmited the data such as static pressure, differential pressure, realtime gas flow, net flow, yesterday flow, cummulative flow, and temperature to display on the website.

SCADAPack 334 Automatic Shut Off System

The system kill 2 oil field locations (Epley C and Epley A) based on the oil level on each tank around the 2 locations. For example, when Epley A tank# 1 reaches 14 ft 6 inches, the brain (located at Epley C location) will send a wireless signal to Epley A location to shut down Epley A site. The kill limit 14ft 6 inches can also be adjusted remotely
