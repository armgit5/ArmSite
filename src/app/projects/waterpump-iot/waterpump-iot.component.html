NodeMCU Water Pump Controller

NodeMCU is used to communicate to an Ionic3 app via Firebase realtime database in the cloud so that the water pump can be controlled from anywhere in the world. * NodeMCU used to be able to communicate to Firebase in real time. Since there was a new update in Firebase, now NodeMCU gets and checks the on/off data from Firebase every 5 seconds. From this link you will find the source codes for NodeMCU and Ionic3 Application (https://github.com/armgit5/waterpump/)



Here is also the circuit of 4 channel relay module that takes 5VDC signal from NodeMCU to control 220VAC to turn on and off the water pump.
